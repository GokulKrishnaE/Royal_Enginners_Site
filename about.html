About Us

Royal Engineers (RE) is a turnkey solution provider for Airconditioning application, exclusive dealer for Samsung India Electronics Private Ltd.

RE provide state of the art air conditioning and ventilation solutions to premium residential and large commercial spaces, meeting the specific requirements of your unique environment with an emphasis on energy efficiency, practicality and commercial viability. RE was formed with the expressed aim of being able to offer a complete, more efficient approach to the traditional method of managing a HVAC project. We have been able to achieve consistent growth in Engineering industry with a philosophy of delivering high quality Engineering and Services to clients cost effectively. Our engineers are skilled to understand your specific requirements to develop a tailormade solution.

We work with all the major air conditioning manufacturers pushing the latest solutions, with the manufacturers often leveraging our expertise to assist them in bringing new products into the market place. Our passion for engineering runs through our veins, we love designing of air conditioning and ventilation systems to provide value engineered solutions. We care about improving indoor air quality, providing heat recovery where possible. Our knowledge and expertise to identify the most cost efficient and practical solution to create a more productive working environment, is second to none.

Our organization has sufficient service personals on the pay rolls and few Sub contractors for executing the works. Our services have been extended for 24 hours / 365 days to the customers, who have opted for this service. We have separate service points at Thiruvanathapuram, Ernakulam, Thrissur and Kozhikode
